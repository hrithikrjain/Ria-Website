OUR STORY

Born from a love of craft

Handcrafted jewellery for India's modern bride

Crafted for your celebration

Ria Art Jewellery is a registered Mumbai-based jewellery brand creating handcrafted imitation jewellery for India's modern brides and festive occasions. Founded with the belief that every woman deserves to feel adorned and celebrated — without compromise.

We design for the woman who wants to walk into her mehendi, her sangeet, her wedding day, and every festive celebration with confidence and grace. Our jewellery carries the warmth of skilled hands, the beauty of Indian heritage, and a commitment to quality that outlasts any occasion.

From our studio in Malad West, Mumbai, we craft each piece with an eye for detail that most imitation jewellery brands overlook — anti-tarnish finishes, nickel-free plating, and a Lifetime Guarantee on all our beads.

Craftsmanship
Every piece is handcrafted by skilled artisans. We believe in the beauty of human hands — no two pieces are ever exactly the same.
Celebration
Our jewellery is designed for life's most meaningful moments — and every festive day in between. We celebrate the occasions that matter to you.
Trust
Nickel-free, anti-tarnish, and backed by our Lifetime Guarantee on beads. We stand behind every piece we create — that is the Ria Art promise.
