CARE & MAINTENANCE

Care Guide

How to keep your jewellery beautiful for years to come

Lifetime Guarantee on Beads

All beads in our jewellery are covered by our Lifetime Guarantee. They will not fade, crack, or discolour under normal wearing conditions.

01
Storage

Store each piece in the provided velvet pouch or a soft cloth bag. Keep jewellery away from moisture and humidity. Store pieces separately — stacking can cause scratches and tangling. Keep in a dry, cool place away from direct sunlight.

02
Cleaning

Use only a soft, dry cloth to gently wipe your jewellery after each use. Do not use tissue paper — it can be abrasive. Never use harsh chemicals, jewellery cleaning liquids, or ultrasonic cleaners on imitation jewellery, as these can strip the plating.

03
What to Avoid

Avoid spraying perfume, deodorant, or hairspray directly onto jewellery — these chemicals accelerate tarnishing. Do not expose pieces to swimming pools, the sea, or any water. Avoid extended exposure to sweat. Do not sleep with jewellery on.

04
Plating Care

Always handle your jewellery with dry, clean hands. Our anti-tarnish plating significantly extends the life of each piece. Oils and moisture from hands are the primary cause of plating wear — a quick wipe after wearing goes a long way.

05
When to Remove

Always remove your jewellery before bathing, swimming, or washing hands. Remove before applying makeup, moisturiser, or sunscreen. Take off jewellery before heavy exercise or any activity involving water or sweat.

06
Bead Care

Our beads are covered by a Lifetime Guarantee. To clean beaded jewellery, use only a barely damp soft cloth — never submerge in water. Dry immediately and thoroughly. Store beaded pieces lying flat to avoid stress on the thread.

Quick Reference: Do & Don't

DO ✓
  • ✓ Wipe with soft dry cloth after use
  • ✓ Store in pouch separately
  • ✓ Handle with dry hands
  • ✓ Wear as the last step when dressing
  • ✓ Remove before bed
DON'T ✗
  • ✗ Spray perfume on jewellery
  • ✗ Wear while swimming or bathing
  • ✗ Use chemical cleaners
  • ✗ Wear during heavy exercise
  • ✗ Store pieces together loosely
