How to Measure Your Bangle Size

Thread method (most accurate):

1. Bring all four fingers together with your thumb touching your little finger (as if slipping on a bangle).

2. Wrap a piece of thread around the widest part of your closed hand.

3. Mark where the thread meets itself and measure the length in centimetres.

4. Divide by π (3.14) to get your inner diameter, then match to the chart below.

Tip: Add 0.2–0.3 cm for comfort if you prefer a looser fit.
