Our Story

Where Tradition Meets Artistry

Ria Art Jewellery was born from a deep love for Indian craft traditions. Based in the heart of Malad West, Mumbai, we create premium imitation jewellery that captures the grandeur of real gold — without the price.

Every piece in our collection is handcrafted by skilled karigars who have dedicated their lives to the art of jewellery making. From the intricate Jadau work on our bridal sets to the delicate bead-stringing of our Ghrom Malas — each creation tells a story of patience and passion.
